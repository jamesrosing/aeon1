Meta Visceral at Lévy Gorvy
London, June & July 2021

An exhibition design for Lévy Gorvy’s outpost on Old Bond Street. The exhibition is called Meta Visceral; it featured a selection of works about the body by artists including Louise Bourgeois, Bruce Nauman, Yves Klein and Carol Rama. We introduced a subtle and tactile velvet ribbon to wrap around the galleries as a backdrop to the artworks. The exhibition space is on Old Bond Street, and the show took place during June and July 2021.
